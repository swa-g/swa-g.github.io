Welcome to my Official Website

Hello, and thank you for visiting my website! I am Dr. Swarnendu Ghosh, an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AIML) at the Institute of Engineering & Management, and an Erasmus Mundus Fellow at the University of Évora, Portugal. My research spans deep learning, neural networks, and their applications across various domains, including medical imaging, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Through this platform, I aim to share my academic work, publications, and contributions to the evolving fields of artificial intelligence and machine learning. I believe in the power of knowledge exchange and collaboration, and I hope this site serves as a valuable resource for students, researchers, and professionals in the field.

Thank you for your interest, and I look forward to engaging with the community and advancing these exciting technologies together.
